## Brief

A clinical endpoint where no residual invasive cancer is detected in the breast and lymph nodes at the time of surgery after neoadjuvant therapy.

## Why this matters

Achieving pCR after neoadjuvant therapy is a strong prognostic indicator for improved long-term outcomes and is a primary endpoint in many clinical trials for early-stage breast cancer.
