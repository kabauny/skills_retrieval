## Brief

A pre-surgical therapy administered to patients to shrink tumors, eliminate microscopic disease, and assess treatment response before definitive surgical intervention.

## Why this matters

Neoadjuvant therapy is a critical strategy in oncology, aiming to downstage tumors, guide subsequent adjuvant therapy, and provide early prognostic information, especially in breast cancer.
