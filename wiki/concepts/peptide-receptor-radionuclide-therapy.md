# Peptide Receptor Radionuclide Therapy

> ⚠️ **Auto-generated stub** from a UI-driven grounded search. Verify and expand before relying on this for clinical decisions. The agent ingest workflow can promote this stub to a full entity/concept page.

## Brief

A targeted radiation therapy approach that uses radiolabeled somatostatin analogs (e.g., Lutetium Lu 177 dotatate) to deliver radiation directly to tumor cells that are somatostatin receptor-positive (SSTR-positive).

## Why this matters

PRRT offers a systemic, targeted radiation option for neuroendocrine tumors like paraganglioma that express somatostatin receptors, aiming to control disease progression and manage symptoms.
