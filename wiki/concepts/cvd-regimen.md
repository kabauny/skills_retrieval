## Brief

A combination chemotherapy regimen comprising cyclophosphamide, vincristine, and dacarbazine, widely used as cytotoxic chemotherapy for advanced paraganglioma, especially in patients with high tumor burden or rapidly progressive disease.

## Why this matters

The CVD regimen is an established and frequently utilized systemic chemotherapy option for advanced paraganglioma, providing a standard approach for disease control when targeted therapies or radiopharmaceuticals are not suitable or sufficient.
