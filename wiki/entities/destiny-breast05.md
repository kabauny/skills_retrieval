# DESTINY-Breast05

## Overview

Phase 3 randomized trial of [[trastuzumab-deruxtecan]] (T-DXd) vs [[trastuzumab-emtansine]] (T-DM1) as adjuvant therapy for high-risk HER2+ early breast cancer with residual invasive disease after neoadjuvant treatment. Topline results presented at ESMO 2025 and SABCS 2025; **practice-changing**, expected to supersede T-DM1 as the adjuvant standard pending FDA action (PDUFA July 7, 2026).

## Key facts

- **Population:** HER2+ early breast cancer with high-risk residual invasive disease after neoadjuvant therapy (the same setting addressed by [[katherine-trial]]) [[mrd-her2-breast-cancer-residual-disease-2026]].
- **Design:** randomized to T-DXd vs T-DM1 (KATHERINE schedule, 14 cycles) [[mrd-her2-breast-cancer-residual-disease-2026]].
- **Primary endpoint:** invasive disease-free survival (IDFS).
  - **HR 0.47** (53% reduction in IDFS event risk vs T-DM1) [[mrd-her2-breast-cancer-residual-disease-2026]]
  - **3-year IDFS: 92.4% (T-DXd) vs 83.7% (T-DM1)** [[mrd-her2-breast-cancer-residual-disease-2026]]
- **Subgroup consistency:** benefit consistent across all patient subsets including ER/PR positive vs negative — directly relevant to HR+/HER2+ patients in Q3-style scenarios [[mrd-her2-breast-cancer-residual-disease-2026]].
- **CNS endpoint:** brain metastasis-free interval **HR 0.64 (95% CI 0.35–1.17), 36% reduction** with T-DXd vs T-DM1 [[destiny-breast05-april-2026-update]]. CI crosses 1.0 — directional finding, not statistically definitive at conventional threshold but consistent with T-DXd's known CNS activity.
- **Overall survival:** favorable trend, **immature** as of late 2025 / early 2026 reporting; OS is a secondary endpoint not yet specifically updated [[destiny-breast05-april-2026-update]].
- **Safety: ILD/pneumonitis is the principal toxicity signal.** T-DXd 9.6% (**including 2 Grade 5 events**) vs T-DM1 1.6% (**0 Grade 5 events**) [[destiny-breast05-april-2026-update]]. Trial protocol included **proactive serial low-dose chest CT** for early ILD detection [[destiny-breast05-april-2026-update]]. **No incremental ILD risk with concurrent radiotherapy** [[destiny-breast05-april-2026-update]].
- **Primary publication:** New England Journal of Medicine, following the ESMO 2025 oral presentation [[destiny-breast05-april-2026-update]]. Replaces the prior wiki citation chain that relied on press releases and meeting abstracts.
- **Regulatory:** FDA breakthrough therapy designation December 2025; sBLA priority review March 2026; **PDUFA target July 7, 2026** [[mrd-her2-breast-cancer-residual-disease-2026]].
