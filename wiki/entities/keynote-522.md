# KEYNOTE-522 trial

## Overview

Phase 3 randomized double-blind trial that established **neoadjuvant + adjuvant pembrolizumab combined with chemotherapy** as standard of care for high-risk early-stage [[triple-negative-breast-cancer]]. Primary endpoints: pathologic complete response (pCR) and event-free survival (EFS); 5-year OS endpoint met at ESMO 2024 (HR 0.66). Foundational reference for all TNBC adjuvant decisions including the Q5 case.

## Key facts

- **Population:** high-risk early-stage TNBC — Stage II–III, T1cN1–2 or T2–4N0 [[tnbc-keynote-522-irae-rechallenge-2026]].
- **Design:**
  - **Neoadjuvant phase (8 cycles):** [[pembrolizumab]] (or placebo) + carboplatin + paclitaxel × 4 cycles → [[pembrolizumab]] (or placebo) + AC (doxorubicin/cyclophosphamide) × 4 cycles
  - **Surgery**
  - **Adjuvant phase (9 cycles, ~1 year):** [[pembrolizumab]] (or placebo), **regardless of pCR**
- **Primary endpoint results:**
  - **pCR rate:** 64.8% (pembro) vs 51.2% (placebo) — significant improvement [[tnbc-keynote-522-irae-rechallenge-2026]]
  - **5-year OS (ESMO 2024):** 86.6% (pembro) vs 81.7% (placebo), **HR 0.66 (95% CI 0.50–0.87, P=0.0015)** [[tnbc-keynote-522-irae-rechallenge-2026]]
  - **5-year OS in pCR subgroup:** 95.1% vs 94.4% — small absolute benefit (~0.7%); **most of the OS gain is in non-pCR subset**. Critical to Q5 calculus [[tnbc-keynote-522-irae-rechallenge-2026]].
  - **"Road to pCR may matter":** the chemo-IO neoadjuvant phase may establish the immune memory; the adjuvant component's marginal contribution after pCR is uncertain [[tnbc-keynote-522-irae-rechallenge-2026]].
- **Toxicity:**
  - Grade ≥3 [[immune-related-adverse-events]]: 13.0% (pembro) vs 1.5% (placebo) [[tnbc-keynote-522-irae-rechallenge-2026]]
  - GI events ~32% of Grade ≥3 irAEs
  - Real-world adjuvant-phase irAE rate: 18.1%
- **Exploratory biomarkers (December 2024):** TMB and T-cell inflamed 18-gene expression profile (TcellinfGEP) prognostic for pCR/EFS, but pembrolizumab benefit observed regardless. **Not actionable for de-escalation in untreated patients** [[tnbc-keynote-522-irae-rechallenge-2026]]. See [[tumor-mutational-burden]].
- **Trial limitation directly relevant to Q5:** **the trial was not designed to isolate the adjuvant benefit after pCR.** All patients received the full year of adjuvant pembrolizumab; there is no randomized evidence on adjuvant-omission after pCR.
- **Successor / de-escalation trials:** SCARLET (anthracycline-sparing), OptimICE-RD and TROPION-Breast03 (Trop-2 ADCs adjuvantly for residual disease), NCT07327021 (MRI + ctDNA-MRD-guided de-escalation, started Jan 2026) [[tnbc-keynote-522-irae-rechallenge-2026]].
