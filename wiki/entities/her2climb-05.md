# HER2CLIMB-05

## Overview

Phase 3 trial evaluating addition of [[tucatinib]] to trastuzumab + pertuzumab as **first-line maintenance** therapy in **metastatic** HER2+ breast cancer. Presented at SABCS 2025. **Important to disambiguate from any adjuvant claim** — HER2CLIMB-05 does not establish a role for tucatinib in early-stage / residual-disease / MRD+ settings.

## Key facts

- **Population: metastatic HER2+ breast cancer, first-line maintenance phase** (after induction therapy) [[mrd-her2-breast-cancer-residual-disease-2026]].
- **Intervention:** tucatinib added to trastuzumab + pertuzumab maintenance vs trastuzumab + pertuzumab alone.
- **Primary endpoint result:** **8.6-month improvement in progression-free survival** with tucatinib added [[mrd-her2-breast-cancer-residual-disease-2026]].
- **Setting note:** this is **metastatic 1L maintenance**, not adjuvant. Extending these data to MRD+ residual disease post-neoadjuvant TCHP would be **investigational extrapolation**, not evidence-based [[mrd-her2-breast-cancer-residual-disease-2026]].
- **Distinct from HER2CLIMB:** the original HER2CLIMB trial established tucatinib + trastuzumab + capecitabine in advanced HER2+ disease including brain metastases. HER2CLIMB-05 is a separate study in a different metastatic setting [[mrd-her2-breast-cancer-residual-disease-2026]].
