# Tucatinib

## Overview

Oral, HER2-selective tyrosine kinase inhibitor (TKI) with minimal EGFR cross-reactivity. CNS-penetrant, with established activity in HER2+ metastatic breast cancer including patients with brain metastases. **No established adjuvant role** in HER2+ early breast cancer as of April 2026 — use in MRD+ residual disease post-neoadjuvant TCHP would be off-label and investigational.

## Key facts

- **Approved indication:** combination with trastuzumab and capecitabine for advanced/metastatic HER2+ breast cancer (HER2CLIMB regimen), including patients with brain metastases [[mrd-her2-breast-cancer-residual-disease-2026]].
- **HER2CLIMB pivotal data:** improved OS in metastatic HER2+ disease, with consistent benefit in patients with brain metastases [[mrd-her2-breast-cancer-residual-disease-2026]].
- **[[her2climb-05]] (SABCS 2025):** added tucatinib to trastuzumab + pertuzumab as **first-line maintenance** in **metastatic** HER2+ breast cancer — 8.6-month PFS improvement. **This is metastatic 1L maintenance, not adjuvant** — does not extend evidence to early-stage disease [[mrd-her2-breast-cancer-residual-disease-2026]].
- **Adjuvant evidence: none.** No prospective adjuvant trial in HER2+ residual disease or MRD+ early disease has read out as of 2025–2026 [[mrd-her2-breast-cancer-residual-disease-2026]]. Adding tucatinib to T-DM1 or T-DXd in the adjuvant setting is **investigational and off-label**, with no NCCN/ASCO/ESMO recommendation.
- **CNS penetration** is the agent's distinguishing feature — clinically relevant rationale, but not yet supported by adjuvant data, even though [[trastuzumab-deruxtecan]] in [[destiny-breast05]] already showed brain-met risk reduction.
